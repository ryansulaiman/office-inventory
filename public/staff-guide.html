Staff Guide

Office Inventory App

A simple guide to requesting devices, transferring items, and reporting incidents — everything you need to know.

Step 1

How to Log In

No passwords needed — just your PIN.

1
Open the app
Go to the app link shared by your admin on your phone or computer browser.
2
Tap your name
You'll see a list of all staff — tap your own name.
3
Enter your 4-digit PIN
Type your PIN using the number pad. You're in!
Requesting

How to Request a Device

Need a phone, laptop, or any item? Here's how.

1
Go to "Request" tab
Tap the 📋 Request tab at the bottom of the screen.
2
Tap "+ New Request"
Choose the item, enter the quantity, and set an expected return date if you know it.
3
Wait for approval
Your Inventory Assistant will review and assign the exact unit (e.g. PHN-001) to you.
My Items

Viewing Your Assigned Items

See everything that's assigned to you in one place.

Go to the 🎒 My Items tab to see all your devices and items.

Each device shows its unit code (e.g. PHN-001), the date it was assigned, and its return due date.

If your return date has passed, it will show a red ⚠️ OVERDUE warning — please return or request an extension!

✅ Assigned ⏰ Due Soon ⚠️ Overdue
Returning

How to Return a Device

You cannot return a device yourself — here's what to do.

Physically hand the device back to your Inventory Assistant.

They will process the return in the app on their end — the device will then be marked as available again.

You do not need to do anything in the app to return a device.

💡 Need more time? Go to 🎒 My Items, tap "📅 Request Extension" on the device, enter a new date and a reason. Your assistant will approve or deny it.

Transfers

Transferring a Device to a Workmate

Need to pass your device to someone else? Easy.

1
Go to "My Items" tab
Find the device you want to transfer.
2
Tap "🔄 Transfer"
Select one or more devices, then choose which workmate to send them to. Add a note if needed.
3
Workmate accepts or declines
They'll see a notification in their 🔄 Transfer tab and can accept or decline.
Incidents

Reporting a Damaged or Lost Device

Accidents happen — here's how to report them quickly.

1
Go to "Report" tab ⚠️
Tap the Report tab at the bottom of your screen.
2
Tap "+ Report Damaged / Lost"
Choose the device, select whether it's damaged or lost, and describe what happened.
3
Submit
Your Inventory Assistant will be notified and will handle the resolution.
Summary

Quick Reference

Everything at a glance.

✅ You Can
  • Request any item or device
  • See all your assigned items
  • Transfer devices to workmates
  • Request a return date extension
  • Report damaged or lost devices
  • Accept or decline transfers
🚫 You Cannot
  • Return devices yourself in the app
  • Approve your own requests
  • See other staff's items
  • Edit inventory or add items
  • Resolve incidents yourself

❓ Questions? Contact your Inventory Assistant or Admin directly.
